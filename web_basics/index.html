In The Early Days of Mankind's History, Demons, Gods, and Monsters Walked the Earth. Mere Mortals Quaked In Their Presence. Who Would Stand Up And Protect The People From The Unstoppable Tide That Could Not Be Held Back? Those Brave Souls Were...

Pan Flute Warriors

Panflute Warriors date back to the very beginning of mankind's history. Initially they began as way of protecting terrified villagers from ancient gods as demons. A victim of there own success, by the year 298 AD all gods and demons had been slain from this mortal world.

"The End of Suffocation", Rembrandt's painting depicting panfloutist St. George slaying the infamous dragon demon "Gorag the Cropduster". Gorag was the final demon witnessed within recorded history.

Panflute Warriors In the Modern World

Panflute Warriors were long thought to have disappeared from the world until the ground-breaking investigative report published by Jean-Paul Marat in 1776. He revealed panflute warriors now walked the earth as monks, dueling each other to the death in order to maintain their fighting prowess for when the darkness returns.
